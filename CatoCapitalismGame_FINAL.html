How to Play

Each round gives you a real policy challenge with four possible responses. Your society starts in rough shape — four health bars beginning at 65%. If any bar drops below the danger line (25%), the nation collapses. After each choice, a breaking news headline appears, real data is shown, and a historical figure shares their view.

😊Citizen Happiness

How satisfied and secure residents feel day-to-day. Drops when policies create instability, shortages, or lost rights.

💰Economic Health

The strength of jobs, growth, and prosperity. Sensitive to taxes, regulation, and government spending.

📚Education Quality

How well schools prepare young people. Affected by competition, funding allocation, and government control.

🗽Individual Freedom

The degree to which citizens can live, speak, and work without government interference. Fragile once eroded.
