Privacy Notice

Freedom Founder · Last updated July 13, 2026

This notice explains what information Freedom Founder collects, how it is used, and how to review or delete it. The game is an educational project of the Cato Institute, Center for Educational Freedom. It is designed for classroom and general audiences and is intended for players ages 13 and older.

The short version

The game itself collects nothing. You can play the entire game with no account and no personal information. All of your progress stays in your own browser.

The only feature that stores anything on our server is the optional "save my progress" account, which asks for a display name and a numeric PIN. That is it. No email, no real name, no address, no location, no photos, and no advertising trackers are ever collected.

What we collect (only if you create an optional account)

  • A display name you choose. We ask you to use a first name or nickname, not a full name.
  • A numeric PIN you choose, so only you can reopen your saved game. The PIN is stored as a one-way hash, not as plain text.
  • Your in-game progress, such as your city's stats, score, and items, so you can continue later.

If you never create an account, none of the above leaves your device.

What we do not collect

  • No email addresses, phone numbers, or mailing addresses.
  • No location or GPS data.
  • No photos, video, or audio.
  • No advertising identifiers, and no third-party advertising or analytics trackers.
  • No open chat. In shared classroom worlds, players can only send a small set of pre-written phrases.

How the information is used

Account information is used for one purpose only: to save your game and let you sign back in to continue it. It is not sold, rented, or shared with advertisers, and it is not used to build a profile of you.

Where it is stored

When a teacher runs the game on a local classroom network, nothing is saved to a server at all. When the game is hosted online, optional accounts are stored in a secured database (Upstash Redis) reached over an encrypted connection. Access is limited to the values you provide.

How long we keep it

Optional accounts are kept only while they are in use. An account with no activity for 90 days is automatically deleted, along with the saved progress attached to it.

Your choices and rights

  • Play without an account. The account feature is entirely optional.
  • Review or delete your data. You can ask us to show or delete the information tied to a display name at any time using the contact below.
  • Parents and guardians may make the same review or deletion request on behalf of their child.

Children

Freedom Founder is intended for players ages 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Because the game can be used in classrooms, we deliberately keep data collection minimal: the optional account stores only a chosen display name, a hashed PIN, and game progress. If you believe a child under 13 has created an account, contact us using the details below and we will delete it.

Changes to this notice

If this notice changes, the "last updated" date above will change with it.
