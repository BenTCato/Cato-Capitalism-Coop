The Policy Game

Can you keep your society thriving?

You're in charge. Ten real policy crises will test your leadership. Every decision shapes your nation's happiness, economy, education, and freedom. History's greatest thinkers will weigh in on every choice you make.

How to Play

Each round presents a real policy challenge. Choose from four approaches reflecting how different economic systems have historically responded. Your four health bars begin at 100% — if any drops below the danger line (25%), society collapses and the game ends. Public trust is fragile: bad policy hits hard and good policy restores it slowly. Read the newspaper headline between questions, review the data chart, and see what history's thinkers say about your choice.
